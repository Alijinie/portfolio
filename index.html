A Journey from Curiosity to Creation

My fascination with digital creation began with a Windows 95 Toshiba Satellite. After my dad taught me the basics to keep me from tampering, a lifelong passion was ignited. Today, that curiosity has evolved into a professional skillset. I help small businesses and side-hustlers stand out with compelling motion graphics and polished websites.

As a self-taught developer now honing my skills at GoMyCode Hackerspace Ikeja, I blend technical precision with creative storytelling. My ability to translate—both linguistically in French, Swahili, and Arabic, and visually through animation—allows me to connect brands with their audiences in powerful ways.
